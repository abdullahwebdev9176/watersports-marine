Standard Maintenance

Keep your boat running at its best with our comprehensive maintenance services. From oil changes and engine checks to full system diagnostics, our experienced mechanics ensure every component is operating smoothly and efficiently. Plus, we provide free digital vehicle inspections with every service, complete with photos and notes, so you can stay informed about your boat’s condition.

100-Hour & Annual Maintenance

Regular service is key to keeping your boat performing reliably year after year. Our certified technicians perform thorough 100-hour and annual maintenance for boat owners across Hampton Roads, inspecting and servicing every vital system to ensure safety, efficiency, and peace of mind on the water.

Fiberglass & Gelcoat Repair

From dock scratches to storm damage, our skilled technicians restore your boat’s beauty and strength. We handle everything from minor cosmetic repairs to major fiberglass and gelcoat restoration, ensuring your vessel looks great and performs like new.

Engine Repowers

When it’s time for more power or better efficiency, trust our experts for a professional engine repower. As an authorized Yamaha and Suzuki service center, Sports Marine can remove your old motor, install a new one, and handle all calibration and rigging, getting you back on the water with improved performance and reliability.

Electronics Installation

Upgrade your boat with the latest technology for navigation, entertainment, and safety. We install GPS systems, fish finders, sonar, radar, stereo systems, and more. Custom-fitted and professionally wired to enhance your boating experience.

Trailer Repair

Keep your trailer road-ready with our full range of repair and maintenance services. From wheel bearing replacements and brake repairs to frame adjustments and lighting fixes, we ensure your trailer is safe, reliable, and ready for your next adventure.

Winterization Services

Protect your boat during the off-season with our complete winterization package. We’ll drain systems, stabilize fuel, and secure your engine and components against freezing temperatures—so your boat stays in top shape and is ready to launch when spring arrives.

Bottom Painting

Give your boat lasting protection and a refreshed look with our professional bottom painting services. We use high-quality marine coatings to prevent algae and barnacle buildup, reduce drag, and extend the life of your hull, keeping your vessel performing efficiently and looking sharp.
