What is a Repower?

A repower means replacing your boat’s outboard engine and related rigging to restore or enhance performance. Even well-maintained engines lose efficiency over time, and repowering is a smart way to boost power, reliability, and fuel economy without buying a new boat. At Sports Marine Center, our expert technicians handle the entire process—from removal to installation and system integration—ensuring your vessel runs like new with the latest technology and precision workmanship.
