Hassle-Free Boat Selling & Upgrading Made Simple

At Sports Marine Center, we make buying, selling, and trading boats simple and stress-free. Whether you’re ready to upgrade to a new model or need to sell your current boat, our consignment and trade programs are designed to help you get the most value while keeping the process smooth and transparent.

Start with a valuation

Trade-In Your Boat

Looking to upgrade? We accept trade-ins on boats, motors, and trailers, giving you instant equity toward your next purchase. Our team evaluates your vessel fairly and offers a competitive trade-in value, making it easy to step into a new boat without the hassle of selling privately.

Boat Consignment

If you’re not ready to trade, consigning your boat with us is a smart way to sell it quickly and efficiently. We handle marketing, inquiries, showings, and sales, giving your boat maximum exposure to buyers while you focus on enjoying your time on the water.
