Why Choose a Center Console Boat?

Center console fishing boats are popular among both anglers and recreational boaters, for a variety of reasons.

  • Unmatched Fishing Capability with 360° Deck Access

    Center console boats offer full walkaround access for fishing from any angle. Built-in rod holders, bait stations, and live wells create a seamless fishing experience.

  • Spacious, Open Layout for Gear and Guests

    An open deck design provides plenty of room for gear and seating, with built-in storage, rod lockers, and live wells to keep everything organized.
